Investigation Overview¶

I would like to give an overall look into Ford GoBike System. Our presentation will be mostly over visualizations.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset is downloaded from https://s3.amazonaws.com/baywheels-data/index.html as seperate months and other dates are available. I will focus on the first 10 months of 2019 data. Sadly our data does not contain any information about the users. Therefore we had to focus on the usage of the system.

Trip Duration¶

There are trips up to 1400 minutes. However I saw that most of the trips are shorter than 60 minutes. We saw above that average trip duration was 809 seconds (13.5 minutes). The graph below shows that most of the trips are less than 20 minutes.

Trip Durations¶

This shows that people take longer trips during weekends.

Shortest trips are done during weekdays at early rush hour when people are going to work. We don't see this pattern in the evenings. Maybe people are more energetic in the early hours and the traffic congestion is low, alloing them to reach their destinations faster.
